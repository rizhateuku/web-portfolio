RT Work About Resume

I'm Rizha,

a seasoned product designer based in Jakarta. For the last four years I've worked on GoFood at Gojek, designing for the kind of scale where a misplaced button can confuse hundreds of thousands of people before lunch. Before that I freelanced motion and UI work, co-founded a fintech startup that raised a pre-seed round before the runway ran dry, and built a small brand-focused design agency on the side. Seven years in, what I care about hasn't really changed — I want to make products that respect the user's time, money, and attention, and that hold up when the easy answer doesn't. I think a lot about trust, why people abandon flows, and how to design through the messy middle between strategy decks and what actually ships. When I'm not designing, I play padel, follow space missions a little too closely, and plan trips I almost go on.

Timeline

Covid hits, challenging times ahead. Building a creative lab, design agency on the side.

2022

Runway's done, it's not surviving. Time to survive for yourself. Joined Gojek.

Now

Still wandering, but aimfully.
